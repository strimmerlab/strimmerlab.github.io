Supervision of student projects:

I am offering student projects on all levels:

  1. BSc projects are single term, taking place in Semester 2 in Year 3.
  2. MMath projects are double term, taking place in Year 4.
  3. MSc theses are written after the exams (June-September).

Typically, the topic of the thesis is to study a recently proposed statistical or machine learning method and to apply it to data. The focus may vary from theory to computational experiments to applied analysis, depending on the interest of the student.
