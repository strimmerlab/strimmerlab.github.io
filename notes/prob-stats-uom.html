About This Page:

This page provides an overview of the Probability and Statistics group of the Department of Mathematics at the University of Manchester, UK.

The reason for the existence of this page is to collect information about the group all in one place (this info is otherwise dispersed across official web sites).

Research Themes:

The Probability and Statistics group is involved in the following departmental research themes:

  1. Statistics, inverse problems, uncertainty quantification and data science,
  2. Probability, financial mathematics and actuarial science and
  3. Mathematics in the life sciences.
