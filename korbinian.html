I am Chair / Professor in Statistics at the University of Manchester. I work on problems in biomedical data science and develop corresponding statistical methods and computational tools, with special focus on high-dimensional data.

Here is the "meet our academics" interview with me on the student blog of the School of Mathematics.

I have been selected by Thomson Reuters as "Highly Cited Researcher" in the field of computer science in the years 2017, 2016, 2015 and 2014, taking into account publications from 2002 to 2015.
