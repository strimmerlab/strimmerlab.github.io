I am Chair / Professor in Statistics at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Manchester. I am interested in principles of statistical learning and machine intelligence and I develop corresponding methods, models and computational tools with special focus on application to high-dimensional biomedical data.

Here is the "meet our academics" interview with me on the student blog of the Department of Mathematics.

I am a member of the Statistics section in the Department of Mathematics.
