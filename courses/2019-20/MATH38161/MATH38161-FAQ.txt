Frequently Asked Questions


Q1) I am a level 4 student.  Can I register for this module?

No. This module is a level 3 course and is intended for level 3 students only!  For level 4 students and above there is the module MATH48061 - Multivariate Statistics.


Q2) Do we get a mock exam?

No, there will not be a mock exam.   However, the exam questions for last year's course (2018/19) are available on Blackboard.

Q3) Is the exam this year open book or closed book?

The exam will be close booked.  This means you cannot bring any notes with you.
