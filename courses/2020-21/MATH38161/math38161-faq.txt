Frequently Asked Questions


1) What is the structure of the exam?

The will be four questions with subquestions.  All four questions are mandatory, and each is worth 20 marks so the maximum total marks achievable in the exam are 80.


2) Which part of the curriculum are relevant for the exam?
 
Everything is relevant with the exception of neural networks and GPs (Week 11).


3) Do I need R in the exam?

Yes, you will need R in the exam.
