Frequently Asked Questions


1) Which part of the curriculum are relevant for the exam?
 
Everything is relevant with the exception of neural networks and GPs (
Semester Week 12 - Course Week 11).


2) Will the exam be "open book"?

The exam (January 2023) will be "partially open book".  This means you are allowed to bring a single sheet of A4 paper, with your own notes typed or handwritten on both
sides.  No other restricted materials are allowed into the exam.

The resit exam (August 2023) will be "closed book".  This means that you cannot bring any materials to the resit.
