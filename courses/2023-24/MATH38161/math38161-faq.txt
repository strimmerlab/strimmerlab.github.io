Academic Year 2023/24 - Semester 1

MATH38161 - Multivariate Statistics and Machine Learning 
Frequently Asked Questions


1) Which part of the curriculum are relevant for the exam?
 
Everything is relevant with the exception of neural networks and GPs (
Semester Week 12 - Course Week 11).


2) Will the exam be "open book"?

The exam (taking place in January 2024) will be "partially open book".
This means you are allowed to bring a single sheet of A4 paper, with your own
notes typed or handwritten on both sides.  No other restricted materials are
allowed into the exam.
