Overview of teaching:

I teach Mathematics and Computer Science students. My current modules are "Statistical Methods" (Year 2) and "Multivariate Statistics and Machine Learning" (Year 3). I have written corresponding lecture notes that can be found here:

  • Multivariate Statistics and Machine Learning (MATH38161)
  • Statistical Methods: Likelihood, Bayes and Regression (MATH20802)

I also supervise BSc, MMath and MSc student projects in Statistics.

Please see below for the individual course home pages with time tables and links to further materials.
